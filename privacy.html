Information We Collect

"sky story" collects your device's location information (latitude and longitude) while you are using the app. This information is required to provide localized weather updates tailored to your current location.

We do not collect other personal data such as names, email addresses, or usage information unrelated to location.

How We Use Your Location Data

We use your location data solely to fetch weather information from a trusted third-party weather service, the OpenWeather API. Your geographic coordinates are transmitted securely over HTTPS to OpenWeather to provide accurate, localized weather forecasts.

We do not use location data for advertising, marketing, analytics, or any other purpose beyond providing weather information.

Data Sharing and Security

Your location data is shared only with OpenWeather as described above. We do not share your location data with any other third parties.

All data transmitted to OpenWeather is encrypted in transit using HTTPS to protect against interception.

Your Control and Choices

You have control over whether you share your location with "sky story." The app will request your permission before accessing location services.

You can choose to deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings. Without location access, the app’s weather functionality will be limited.

No Collection of Other Personal Data

Other than location data as described above, "sky story" does not collect or store any personal data or usage information.
